About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with deep expertise in Large Language Models (LLMs), working at the intersection of research and real-world applications. My focus is on designing, fine-tuning, and deploying LLM-driven systems that power enterprise solutions, enhance automation, and improve decision-making.

At Celonis, I have developed multiple LLM-based products, including an Automatic Prompt Generation module that simplifies prompt engineering for business users. I also built a data governance system for Celonis’ Copilot, ensuring high-quality retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) outputs. My work has been recognized in both academic conferences (SIGMOD) and industry events (Celosphere 2024).

My experience extends beyond product development. I have worked on LLM research, contrastive learning, and large-scale representation learning. With a background in computational data science from Carnegie Mellon University and prior research experience in deep learning, I am passionate about contributing the Generative AI field and making products with LLMs.
